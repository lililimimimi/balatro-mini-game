SmileyFace

Activates: On Scored

§

FlowerPot

x3 Mult if poker hand contains a ♦Diamond card, ♣Club card, ♥Heart card, and ♠Spade card.

Activates: Independent

§

FourFingers

All Flushes and Straights can be made with 4 cards.

Example 1: 4♦ 5♣ 6♦ 7♠ J♣ is a straight (J♣ not counted).

Example 2: 3♦ 7♦ 9♦ A♦ is a flush.

Example 3: 6♥ 7♥ 8♥ 9♥ is a straight flush.

Example 4: 3♥ 4♥ 5♣ 6♥ J♥ is a straight flush, since it contains both a straight and a flush.

Activates: N/A

§

Shortcut

Allows Straights to be made with gaps of 1 rank

(e.g. 10 8 6 5 3)

Activates: N/A

§

Mime

Retrigger all card held in hand abilities.

Activates: On Held

§

Pareidolia

All cards are considered face cards.

Activates: N/A

§

Splash

Every played card counts in scoring.

Activates: N/A

§

SockAndBuskin

Retrigger all played face cards.

Activates: On Scored

§

SmearedJoker

♥Hearts and ♦Diamonds count as the same suit, ♠Spades and ♣Clubs count as the same suit.

Activates: N/A

§

Blueprint

Copies the ability of Joker to the right.

Activates: Same as copied
